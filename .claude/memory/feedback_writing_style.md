Decifer's mission is to make sense of any industry for the common person. In trading: cut the noise, give a usable signal in plain language.

**Rule:** Write like a human, not a bot. Get to the point. No em dashes (—). No filler phrases.

**Why:** Amit has stated this explicitly and will not repeat it. Every build must follow this before shipping.

**How to apply:**
- Replace em dashes with a period, comma, or rewrite the sentence
- No "it usually signals", "typically indicates" bot-speak — say what it means directly
- Short sentences. Active voice. Concrete nouns.
- Bad: "When both sides move at once it usually signals a known upcoming event — earnings, FDA decision, or macro announcement."
- Good: "Both sides spiking together usually means a catalyst is coming: earnings, an FDA decision, or a big macro print."
